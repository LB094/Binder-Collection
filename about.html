About

A place for Pokémon TCG collectors to browse, explore and discover card sets.

What is Binder Collection?

Binder Collection is a website to look through sets of Pokémon cards. Often people collect every single card from a certain set, also known as creating a "Master Set". Each set has every card in order exactly how it would be put into a binder or collection.

What's coming?

In the future, Binder Collection wants to implement a virtual binder with a progress bar. This would allow users to log in and keep track of their personal collection, and show how much of each set has been collected.
