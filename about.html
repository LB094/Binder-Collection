About

Binder Collection is a website to look through sets of Pokemon cards. Often people collect every single card from a certain set, also known as creating a "Master Set". Each set has every card in order exactly how it would be put into a binder or collection.

In the future Binder Collection wants to implement a virtual Binder with a progress bar. This would allow users to log in and keep track of their personal collection and show how much of each set has been collected.
